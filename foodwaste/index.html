What's Food Waste?

Food waste is when we throw away food that we could have eaten. This happens in many places, from farms to our homes.

Why Food Waste is Bad

Food waste hurts our planet, wastes money, and means some people don't have enough to eat. It makes more pollution and uses up resources we need.

How to Waste Less Food

Plan your meals, keep food fresh, use leftovers in new ways, and make compost from food scraps. These easy steps can help reduce food waste at home.

Food Waste in Schools

One of the main reasons for food wastage in schools is that children do not eat the food they buy from the buffet or bring from home and throw it away. We can form groups in the school to monitor the dining hall to prevent food wastage as much as possible.

Another thing we can do is for the school cafeteria to try to adjust the size of the food portions so that no one overeats and wastes food. Of course, it is important for the children themselves to be responsible and try not to waste food.

What Can We Do?

  • Form groups to monitor the dining hall and prevent food waste.
  • Adjust food portion sizes in the school cafeteria.
  • Educate students on the importance of not wasting food.

Food Waste Numbers

33% of food is wasted around the world

About one-third of all food made for people to eat is lost or wasted. That's about 1.3 billion tons every year!
